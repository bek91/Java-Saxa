The Midnight Mug

Lauinger Library, Second Floor,
Washington, DC 20007

The perfect place to stop for a quick break or extended study session, MUG is there to provide you with efficient service and quality goods. Affectionately known as the "little cart with the big heart," MUG makes up for its small size with its deep appreciation for its customers, and our team is always looking for new ways to make each day a little bit better for the Georgetown community.

Grounded

Healey Family Student Center, 3700 O St NW
Washington, DC 20007

Since 2016, The Hilltoss has included Grounded, the Corp’s newest coffee shop. Grounded sells coffee, tea, non-espresso based specialty drinks, breakfast toasts, and bagel sandwiches. As a healthy dining option on the south-side of campus, this storefront will fill the gap between Lau and Leo’s offering fresh creations to keep students fueled up and feeling great.

Uncommon Grounds

Edward B. Bunn S.J. Intercultural Center,
Washington, DC 20007

Uncommon Grounds (UG) opened its doors to the Georgetown community in 1995, right across from the University bookstore in the Leavey Center. Whether you are looking for a traditional coffee, tasty latte, or soothing cup of tea, UG has you covered for both early morning and late night necessities.
